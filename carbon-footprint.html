Game Rules & How to Play

üéØ Objective

The goal of Carbon Footprint Simulator is to make sustainable lifestyle choices across different categories to minimize your carbon footprint while learning about environmental impact.

üîÑ How to Play

  1. Select Choices: Click on different options in each category (Transportation, Diet, Housing, Energy)
  2. See Impact: Watch how your choices affect your carbon footprint in real-time
  3. Learn Facts: Read the "Did You Know?" popups to understand the environmental impact of each choice
  4. Optimize: Try different combinations to achieve the lowest possible carbon footprint
  5. Earn Achievements: Unlock sustainability badges by making eco-friendly choices

üìä Scoring System

  • Excellent (Under 4 tons): Minimal environmental impact
  • Good (4-6 tons): Moderate environmental impact
  • Poor (6-8 tons): Noticeable environmental impact
  • Critical (Over 8 tons): Significant environmental damage

Your goal is to maintain an "Excellent" or "Good" environmental status.

üèÜ Achievements

  • Eco-Starter: Begin your sustainability journey
  • Low-Carbon Diet: Choose plant-based or vegetarian options
  • Green Commuter: Select walking, biking, or electric vehicles
  • Energy Saver: Choose eco-homes and renewable energy
  • Eco Master: Optimize all categories for minimal impact

üåç Environmental Impact

The visual environment changes based on your carbon footprint:

  • Low footprint: Clear skies, healthy green trees
  • High footprint: Polluted skies, industrial elements, less vibrant nature

üí° Tips for Success

  • Read the environmental facts to understand why certain choices are better
  • Try to balance choices across all categories
  • Reset and experiment with different combinations
  • Aim for the Eco Master achievement by optimizing all categories

üìà Learning Outcomes

By playing this game, you'll learn:

  • The carbon impact of different transportation methods
  • How dietary choices affect the environment
  • The importance of energy-efficient housing
  • The benefits of renewable energy sources
  • Strategies for reducing your personal carbon footprint
