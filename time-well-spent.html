How Time Well Spent Works

  • Add Activities: Enter what you did today, categorize it, and specify how long it took
  • Categorize: Classify activities as Work (productive tasks), Rest (self-care, relaxation), or Joy (fun, hobbies)
  • Visualize: See your day represented as a "time flower" where each petal is an activity
  • Understand: Get insights about your work-rest-joy balance and emotional time investment
  • Reflect: Use the visualization to understand patterns and make intentional choices about your time

Time Well Spent helps you visualize time emotionally, not just as charts or statistics. See the true balance in your daily life.
