How to Play

  1. You will be presented with a short, ambiguous message (e.g., "Oh, great." or "Thanks a lot.").
  2. Analyze the message and select what you believe is the correct tone from four multiple-choice options.
  3. Click on the button corresponding to your chosen tone (e.g., Sarcastic, Genuine, Angry, Playful).
  4. Get instant feedback showing if you were correct or not, along with a brief explanation of the context.
  5. Each correct guess earns you 1 point.
  6. Click "Next Mystery" to receive a new message and continue playing. See how high you can score!
