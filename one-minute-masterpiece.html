How to Play One-Minute Masterpiece

Game Rules

  • You'll be given a random, common object to draw (like "a key", "a cup", or "a leaf").
  • You have exactly 60 seconds to draw the object.
  • The goal is not perfection but to capture the object's essence.
  • Focus on the most recognizable features rather than details.
  • When time runs out, your drawing is complete - no additions allowed!
  • Share your creation with friends and compare interpretations!

Why It's Fun

One-Minute Masterpiece removes the pressure of "being good at art" and makes drawing about energy, intuition, and fun. The time constraint forces you to focus on what's truly important about each object, often leading to surprisingly creative results!

Tips for Success

  • Start with the basic shape - don't worry about details at first
  • Think about what makes the object recognizable at a glance
  • Use bold, confident strokes rather than hesitant lines
  • Remember: it's about the essence, not perfection!
