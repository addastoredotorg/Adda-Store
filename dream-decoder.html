How to Use the Dream Decoder

  1. Describe Your Dream: In the text box, write a detailed description of your dream. The more vivid and descriptive you are, the more unique your results will be!
  2. Summon the Decoder: Click the "Decode My Dream" button. Our algorithm will analyze your text for common dream archetypes and symbols.
  3. Witness the Art: Behold! A one-of-a-kind, surreal artwork inspired by your dream narrative will appear.
  4. Explore the Symbol Map: Below the art, you will find your personalized "Symbol Map." This breaks down the key elements from your dream and offers insights into their potential psychological meanings.
  5. Reflect & Share: Use the interpretations as a starting point for your own self-reflection.

Remember: This tool is for creative and entertainment purposes. It is not a substitute for professional psychological advice.
