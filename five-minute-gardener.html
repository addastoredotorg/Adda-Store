How to Play The 5-Minute Gardener

Daily Routine (5 Minutes Maximum)

  1. Check Your Garden: Visit once per day to see how your plants have grown
  2. Water Plants: Click on dry plants to water them (each plant needs water once per day)
  3. Remove Weeds: Click on weeds to remove them (prevents plant growth)
  4. Harvest Ready Plants: Collect mature plants to earn gardening points
  5. Plant New Seeds: Use points to plant new varieties in empty garden spots

Mindful Gardening Tips

  • Use your 5-minute session as a mindfulness practice
  • Focus on the calming routine rather than maximizing efficiency
  • Appreciate the simple satisfaction of nurturing growth
  • Let the game be a digital sanctuary from daily stress
