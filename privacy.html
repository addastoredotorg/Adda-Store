Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 18, 2025

Last updated: October 18, 2025. This Privacy Policy explains how AddaStore.fun ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit or interact with our website and services.

1. Summary — in plain language

We build small web tools and games that mostly work without accounts. We collect minimal information (anonymous usage data, cookies, and optionally the email you provide). We do not sell your personal information. This page explains details, user choices, and how to contact us.

2. Information we collect

A. Information you give us

When you contact us, sign up for newsletter, or purchase a product (for example, an art export or PDF), you may provide:

  • Name (optional)
  • Email address
  • Payment or billing details processed by our payment provider (we do not store full card numbers)
  • Content you upload (text, images) for a specific feature or export

B. Information we collect automatically

When you visit AddaStore.fun we may automatically collect:

  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, session length)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies below)

C. Third-party information

We may receive information about you from third-party services you use to interact with our site (for example, payment processors or analytics providers). We only use those third-party services for the limited purposes described below.

3. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services and features
  • Process transactions and provide customer support
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, account messages) and optional newsletters if you subscribe
  • Understand usage and analytics to improve the site
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide essential site functionality and to analyze site usage. Cookies may be:

  • Essential: required for the site to work (session cookies, security tokens)
  • Performance & Analytics: to understand traffic and improve features (e.g., Google Analytics or similar)
  • Functional: to remember preferences (language or UI choices)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies may reduce our ability to improve the site.

5. Third-party services

We may use third-party providers for analytics, payments, hosting, email, and other services (for example: Stripe, PayPal, Google Analytics, email delivery services). Those providers may collect or process data for the limited purposes of providing their services to us. We choose vendors who are trusted and require them to protect your information.

6. Sharing & disclosures

We do not sell personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: vendors that help us run the site (hosting, payments, email)
  • Legal reasons: when required to comply with the law or protect our rights
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets — in which case users will be notified and data transfer rules will apply

7. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Usage and analytics data may be retained in aggregate form for product improvement.

8. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information. To exercise rights or ask questions, contact us at contact@addastore.fun.

If you no longer want to receive marketing emails, you may unsubscribe using the link in any marketing message.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a data incident occurs, we will follow legal notification requirements and notify affected users when required.

10. International transfers

AddaStore.fun is hosted on servers that may be located outside your country. If you are located in the EU/EEA/UK, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Children’s privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Links to other websites

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of sites we do not control. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated where appropriate (e.g., email if you are subscribed).
