About RRB NTPC Exam & Study Tips

Exam Overview

The RRB NTPC (Non-Technical Popular Categories) exam is conducted by Railway Recruitment Boards to recruit staff for various posts in Indian Railways.

  • Stages: CBT 1, CBT 2, Typing Skill Test/Trade Test, Document Verification
  • Subjects: Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness
  • Duration: 90 minutes for CBT 1
  • Questions: 100 questions in CBT 1

Syllabus Highlights

  • Mathematics: Number System, Decimals, Fractions, Ratio & Proportion, Percentage, Time & Work, etc.
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning: Analogies, Classification, Coding-Decoding, Puzzle, Matrix, etc.
  • General Awareness: Current Events, Indian History, Geography, Culture, Polity, etc.
  • Current Affairs: National & International events, Sports, Awards, Books & Authors, etc.

Effective Study Strategies

  • Create a realistic study schedule and stick to it
  • Focus on understanding concepts rather than rote learning
  • Practice previous years' question papers regularly
  • Take regular mock tests to assess your preparation
  • Revise important formulas and concepts frequently
  • Stay updated with current affairs through newspapers and reliable sources
