How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the item cost - The purchase price of the item
  2. Estimate usage - How many times you realistically expect to use the item
  3. Select timeframe - The period over which you'll use the item
  4. Calculate - See the price per use and evaluate if it's worth it

Tips for Accurate Estimates

  • Be realistic about how often you'll actually use an item
  • Consider seasonal items that might only be used part of the year
  • For clothing, estimate based on how often you'll wear it
  • For appliances, consider daily or weekly usage patterns
  • Higher quality items often have more uses over their lifetime
