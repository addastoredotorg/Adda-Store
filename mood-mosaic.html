About Mood Mosaic

Mood Mosaic is a unique visual emotion tracker that transforms your feelings into beautiful 3x3 abstract art patterns. It combines the benefits of journaling with the therapeutic power of art creation.

How It Works

Each day, select colors that represent your emotions and place them on the 3x3 grid. Over time, you'll create a visual diary of your emotional journey that reveals patterns and insights about your mental wellness.

Benefits of Mood Tracking

  • Increased emotional awareness and self-understanding
  • Identification of emotional patterns and triggers
  • Stress reduction through creative expression
  • Visual representation of mental health progress
  • Non-judgmental space for all emotions
  • Beautiful art creation from your emotional journey

Mental Wellness & Emotional Health

Mood Mosaic serves as a digital mental wellness journal that helps users track their emotional states through color-coded visual art. This approach to emotion tracking makes the process engaging and therapeutic.

Art Therapy & Self-Care

By transforming emotions into art, Mood Mosaic incorporates principles of art therapy into daily self-care routines. The simple 3x3 grid makes it accessible while allowing for creative expression.

Privacy-Focused Design

Your emotional data stays securely in your browser. We believe in privacy-first mental health tools that put you in control of your personal information.

How to Use Mood Mosaic

  1. Select a date - Choose the day you want to track emotions for
  2. Choose your emotions - Tap colored tiles from the palette that represent how you're feeling
  3. Build your mosaic - Tap on the 3x3 grid to place tiles and create a visual representation of your emotions
  4. Save your progress - Click "Save Today's Mood" to record your emotional state and unlock sharing options
  5. Share your art - After saving, share your mood mosaic as a JPEG image on social media
  6. Track over time - Return daily to build a beautiful mosaic of your emotional journey
