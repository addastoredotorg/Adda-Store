How to Use Mood Mosaic

  1. Select a date - Choose the day you want to track emotions for
  2. Choose your emotions - Drag colored tiles from the palette that represent how you're feeling
  3. Build your mosaic - Place tiles on the grid to create a visual representation of your emotions
  4. Save your progress - Click "Save Today's Mood" to record your emotional state
  5. Track over time - Return daily to build a beautiful mosaic of your emotional journey

Game Rules & Benefits

  • Consistency Reward: Build longer streaks for more visual satisfaction
  • Pattern Recognition: Identify emotional patterns through color clusters
  • Non-judgmental Tracking: All emotions are valid and contribute to your unique mosaic
  • Art Therapy: The process of creating visual art from emotions has therapeutic benefits
  • Privacy Focused: Your data stays in your browser - no registration required
