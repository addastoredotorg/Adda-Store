    How to Play Focus Dice

    🎯 The Goal

    Break through procrastination by completing quick, randomized focus tasks that help you start working and build momentum.

    ⚡ Basic Rules

    1. Roll the dice when you're struggling to start or maintain focus
    2. Complete the task that appears - no excuses!
    3. Mark it done to track your progress and build streaks
    4. Roll again whenever you need another focus boost

    💡 Pro Tips

    • Use Focus Dice at the start of your work session to build momentum
    • When you hit a productivity wall, roll instead of checking social media
    • Try to maintain your daily streak - consistency builds habits
    • Even "skipped" tasks can provide insight into your resistance patterns
    • Combine with other productivity methods like Pomodoro for best results
