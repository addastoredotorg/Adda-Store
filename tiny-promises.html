How Tiny Promises Works

  1. Make a tiny promise - Choose a 1-minute habit you can realistically complete today
  2. Watch your plant grow - Each kept promise adds a leaf to your plant
  3. Build consistency - Maintain a streak to see your plant flourish with flowers
  4. Be kind to yourself - If you miss a day, your plant loses a leaf but doesn't die
  5. Build self-trust - The real reward is proving to yourself that you keep commitments

Game Rules

  • Each day you can make one tiny promise (1-minute habit)
  • Completing your promise adds a leaf to your plant
  • 5 kept promises in a row makes your plant flower
  • Missing a promise causes one leaf to wilt
  • Your plant never dies completely - you can always regrow it
  • The focus is on consistency, not perfection

Why Tiny Promises Work

Traditional goal-setting often fails because we commit to too much too soon. Tiny Promises uses micro-habits to build self-trust through achievable daily wins. By focusing on small, consistent actions, you develop the identity of someone who keeps commitments to themselves.
