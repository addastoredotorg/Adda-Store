How the Decision Matrix Works

Define your decision clearly to establish context
Identify all factors that influence your decision
Weight each factor by importance (1-5 scale)
List all options you're considering
Rate each option against every factor (1-5 scale)
The matrix calculates weighted scores for each option
The option with the highest score is your data-driven recommendation
Use the results as guidance, but trust your intuition too
