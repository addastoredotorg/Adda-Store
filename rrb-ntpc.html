🎯 Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • You must be a Graduate in any discipline from a recognized university.
  • Crucial Point: You must have passed and received your final degree on or before November 20, 2025.
  • Candidates awaiting their results are NOT eligible.

Age Limit (as on January 1, 2026)

You must be between 18 to 33 years old. This means you must have been born not earlier than 2nd January 1993 and not later than 1st January 2008.

📝 The Recruitment Process

Your path to a railway job will involve these 4 stages:

1

1st Stage CBT

Common for all posts

  • Duration: 90 Minutes (120 mins for scribe users)
  • Questions: 100
  • Marking: Negative marking of ⅓ for each wrong answer
2

2nd Stage CBT

  • Duration: 90 Minutes (120 mins for scribe users)
  • Questions: 120
  • Marking: Negative marking of ⅓ for each wrong answer
3

Skill Test

Depending on the post

  • CBAT: For Station Master & Traffic Assistant
  • CBTST: For Clerk posts (30 WPM English / 25 WPM Hindi)
4

Document Verification & Medical Exam

The final hurdle where your original documents and medical fitness will be verified.

🖥️ How to Apply: A Quick Walkthrough

Create an Account

Go to any official RRB website. Use a personal mobile number and email ID that you have access to, as these cannot be changed later.

Fill the Application

Log in, choose your ONE preferred RRB, and carefully fill in all details. You can indicate your preferences for posts and zones here.

Upload Documents

You need to upload:

  • Your Signature: In running handwriting, with black ink on white paper. BLOCK LETTERS WILL LEAD TO REJECTION.
  • Live Photograph: The system will capture your live photo via webcam during the application process.

Pay the Fee

Pay the application fee online through the available payment methods.

Modify if Needed

You can correct mistakes (except RRB choice and account details) from Nov 23-Dec 2 by paying a ₹250 modification fee.
