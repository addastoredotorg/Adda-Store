Why Media Literacy Matters

Being able to identify bias helps you become a more critical news consumer. It allows you to:

  • Understand different perspectives on the same issue
  • Recognize when information is framed to elicit emotional responses
  • Make more informed decisions based on balanced information
  • Break out of algorithmic echo chambers

Common Bias Indicators

Left-Leaning Cues
  • Focus on systemic inequality
  • Use of "social justice" framing
  • Emphasis on climate change urgency
  • Progressive terminology
Right-Leaning Cues
  • Focus on individual responsibility
  • Use of "traditional values" framing
  • Skepticism of climate policies
  • Conservative terminology
Centrist Cues
  • Balanced presentation of multiple views
  • Neutral, factual language
  • Avoidance of emotionally charged terms
  • Focus on verifiable information
